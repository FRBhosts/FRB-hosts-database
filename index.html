About the project

The goal of this database is to provide an up-to-date overview of currently known FRB hosts. This is a compilation of FRB hosts reported in the literate in addition to new host galaxy identifications and measurements provided by the Fast and Fortunate for FRB follow-up team.
Motivation: Brief intro on FRBs. Still most constraints from host properties. Easy to look up, plot and compare various FRB host galaxy properties.
Acknowledge this work: XX ref. or terms of use: 1) cite the original observational papers of the data you used, 2) for the compilation and new measurements cite XX, and 3) add a footnote to the the webpage FRBhosts_whatever.org
We appreciate any comments on the webpage and contents. Please also feel free to send info about your new FRB hosts if it, for some reason, is not included yet.
